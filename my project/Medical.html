Medical Services

SAR Medical provides solutions to doctors, patients and nurses.
Being a leading Medical Devices provider in almost every segment, what sets it apart is its constant focus on innovation. It offers an extensive range of high technology medical and surgical equipment, devices and services. The business is organized into franchises to focus on target customers and better service.

Bariatric and Metabolic Surgery

Ethicon develops minimally invasive surgical equipment used in sleeve gastrectomy and gastric bypass procedures that can help people conquer morbid obesity and help address other metabolic diseases.

General Surgery & Gynecology

Routinely performed procedures like removal of appendix or gall bladder and gynecological procedures like hysterectomy can be accomplished through the minimally invasive technique using Ethicon’s range of energy devices which cause minimal damage to the tissue and lead to faster recovery.

Wound Closure

Every type of tissue, whether skin, fascia or organ, has its own specialized function and needs. Our Ethicon Wound Closure Portfolio including sutures and topical skin adhesives lets surgeons match the specific wound healing needs of each tissue type to the appropriate closure device, giving surgeons and patients the right solutions for the right procedures.

Hernia Repair

Ethicon’s commitment to advancing hernia repair is reflected in our ongoing investments in evidence, innovative products, and best-in-class professional education.

Cancer Surgery

Cancers of Stomach, Liver, Colon & Rectum, Esophagus, Lung, Head & Neck, Breast etc. are treated surgically using Ethicon products. Cancer surgeons use Ethicon’s precise devices to perform these complex procedures by traditional or minimally invasive approach leading to positive outcomes for the patients.

Bleeding Management

Biosurgery is an area dedicated to aid in surgery by focusing on an array of technologies to minimize intra- and post-operative complications. Solutions comprise innovative, biologically based products for surgical conditions that are often difficult or expensive to manage.

The Orthopaedics business of DePuy Synthes comprises Knee and Hip Reconstruction, Trauma, Spine, sports Medicine and Power Tools categories. It provides comprehensive solutions for advanced surgical intervention associated with the treatment of orthopedic disabilities, disease profiles & trauma care. Collectively, these businesses are focused on equipping surgeons with well-researched products, state-of-the-art instrumentation & advance procedure-product education.

DePuy Synthes Joint Reconstruction

A global leader in joint replacement with a comprehensive portfolio of hip, knee and shoulder replacements, operating room products and bone cement and accessories.

DePuy Synthes Trauma

The global leader in medical devices used to treat orthopaedic trauma. The company's fixation products, including screws, plates, nails and other implants, are used to treat fractures and deformities related to the extremities and pelvis.

DePuy Synthes Power Tools

Offers a comprehensive range of air, electric and battery-driven systems and associated dissections tools. Synthes offers products in three main areas: Large Bone, Small Bone and High Speed.

DePuy Synthes Spine

Offering one of the most comprehensive portfolios of spinal care solutions, treatment options are available in key areas including thoracolumbar, cervical, aging spine, interbody fusion and arthroplasty.

Codman Neuro

We offer a broad portfolio of devices for hydrocephalus management, neuro intensive care and cranial surgery, as well as aneurysm coils, vascular reconstruction devices and other technologies used in the endovascular treatment of cerebral aneurysms and stroke.

DePuy Synthes Mitek Sports Medicine

A global leader in orthopaedic sports medicine devices and products used in the treatment of traumatic and degenerative soft tissue pathologies and joint injuries related to sports and physical activity.

DePuy Synthes CMF

A global leader in cranio-maxillofacial devices, offering a comprehensive portfolio of products for surgeons to treat patients who have sustained conditions affecting their face, head, neck and thorax.

Cardiovascular Disease

Good health depends on blood flowing freely through the entire body. When the heart or blood vessels are not functioning normally, doctors can use tools from Biosense Webster & Ethicon to bring patients back to good health.

Arrhythmias

To treat patients with irregular heartbeats, hospitals and teaching institutions rely on advanced diagnostic, therapeutic and mapping tools from Biosense Webster.

Infection Prevention

The Advanced Sterilization Products portfolio enhances patient care by preventing infections in hospitals. This encompasses adopting a trusted role in Operating Rooms, Intensive Care Units, Central Sterile Supplies Departments and Endoscopy units, and by offering a wide range of innovative sterilization and disinfection products.

Diabetes Care

Managing diabetes effectively means a healthier, happier life. Designed with ongoing input from patients, products from One Touch® help in monitoring of blood glucose levels. This can help patients manage their diabetes. One Touch® helps patients stay healthy and upgrade their quality of life with One Touch® blood glucose monitors and testing strips.
